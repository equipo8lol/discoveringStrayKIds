WHO ARE STRAY KIDS?

Stray Kids (스트레이 키즈) is a South Korean boy group under JYP Entertainment.
 The group consists of 8 members: Bang Chan, Lee Know, Changbin, Hyunjin, Han,
Felix, Seungmin, and I.N. Stray Kids was created through the survival program with the
same name, Stray Kids. Stray Kids debuted on March 25, 2018. As of February 10th, 2022,
they are also signed under Republic Records.
  • International name: Stray Kids
  • Korean name: 스트레이 키즈
  • Origin: South Korea
  • Debut: March 25, 2018
  • Debut album: I’m NOT (mini album)
  • Presentation: ¡Step Out! Hello, We are ¡Stray Kids!
  • Number of members: 8


Pre-Debut
  • Sub-unit 3Racha
    • At the end of 2016, the hip hop trio 3Racha, formed by JYP Entertainment trainees Bang Christopher (Bang Chan), Seo Chang Bin, and Han Ji Sung, with the aliases of CB97, SpearB, and J.One, respectively, was announced. On January 18, 2017, 3Racha officially debuted with their first digital mixtape "J/2017/mixtape". On August 16, 2017, they released their second mixtape, named "3Days", during this time it was announced that the three would formn part of JYP Entertainment's next reality show. On December 20, 2017, 3Racha released their third mixtape titled "Horizon"




    Reality show "Stray Kids"
    In September 2017, JYP Entertainment reveals its plans to debut a new group of kids, with the help of MNET , and broadcast the reality show 'Stray Kids', same name the group received. During the program, participants will have to carry out a series of missions in order to excel in them and not be eliminated. Participants will have to excel in areas such as singing, dancing, rapping, songwriting, production and teamwork. The program began its broadcast in October 2017 and ended in December 2017, where only the final decision would depend on the viewers.

    But before the end of the program, on October 06, 2017, Stray Kids premieres through Vlive the single "Hellevator", a song produced and choreographed by the participants; even, according to Billboard, the song ranked #4 on the Top best-selling k-pop songs in November. In the end, JYP Entertainment decided that Stray Kids would debut with the 9 boys - Bang Chan, Lee Know, Changbin, Hyunjin, Han, Felix, Seungmin, I.N. and Woojin (current former member), and 96% of the public agreed. On January 08, 2018, Stray Kids releases their pre-debut album "Hellevator", with the 7 songs featured during the reality show: "Hellevator", "Grrr", "Young Wings", "YAYAYAYA", "GLOW", "School Life" and "4419". Songs that also features the Stray Kids guys in its composition. The album was #1 on iTunes albums of 7 countries, #2 on Gaon Album Chart and World Album of Billlboard, and according to Gaon Chart, as of March 2021, it has sold 118,921 copies in South Korea. Debut On March 25, 2018, Stray Kids debuts with their first mini album, titled ''I Am NOT''. The album features the songs, ''Not!", "District 9", "Mirror", "Awaken", "Rock (돌)", "Grow Up", "3rd Eye" and "MIXTAPE #1" (CD only). The title track "District 9" got 4,274,649 plays on YouTube in its first 24 hours. The album ''I'm NOT'' reached #1 on iTunes in 14 countries, #4 on Gaon Album Chart and #5 on Billboard's World Album Chart. Moreover, according to Gaon Chart, as of July 2021, the album has recorded a total of 181,971 copies sold in South Korea. And the title track "District 9" ranked #10 on World Digital Song Sales. JAPAN DEBUT: On March 18, 2020, Stray Kids debuts in Japan with the album ''SKZ2020'', an album that features 27 songs.
